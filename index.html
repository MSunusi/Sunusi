About Me

I am an AI researcher and technology leader working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, energy systems, and sustainable development. As Cofounder and CTO of EjazTech.AI, I lead initiatives that develop locally relevant AI solutions while mentoring emerging talent across Africa.

My work focuses on building deep learning systems for real-world applications including agriculture, infrastructure inspection, and energy systems. Beyond research, I actively promote responsible AI adoption through community outreach programs, technical mentorship, and STEM education initiatives for underserved students.

My long-term mission is to democratize access to advanced AI technologies and empower the next generation of innovators across developing regions.

Research Vision

My research vision is to develop trustworthy artificial intelligence systems capable of solving real-world challenges in energy, agriculture, and infrastructure monitoring. I am particularly interested in computer vision systems, data-centric AI approaches, and scalable machine learning methods that operate effectively in underrepresented environments.

Through open-source collaboration, mentorship, and interdisciplinary research, I aim to contribute to a future where advanced AI technologies are accessible and beneficial to communities worldwide.
